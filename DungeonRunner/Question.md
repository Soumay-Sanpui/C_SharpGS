## 🎮 DungeonRun
A console based dungeon game where a hero explores rooms, fights enemies and collects items.


## The Game
 - There a __single system__ managing the game.
 - A hero moves through dungeon rooms one by one
 - Each room can have an enemy, a trap, or loot
 - The hero has health, a weapon and a combat style they can switch
- Enemies vary based on the dungeon theme (Crypt, Volcano etc.)
 - Items picked up __stack their effects__ onto the hero's weapon
 - Every player action is tracked and __can be undone.__
 - Multiple systems (UI, score, loot) react when things happen in the game.
